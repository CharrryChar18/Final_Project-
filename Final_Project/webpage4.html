Charmander was one of the three starter Pokémon in Pokèmon Red and Green, the first game in this franchise. Alongside Bulbasaur and Squirtle, Charmander has an evolutionary line of Charmeleon and Charizard. A fact about Charmander is that it is said that the flame at the end of its tail is its life force. Whether it burns brightly or dimly indicates its health.
